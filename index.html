Course description

This course will cover a range of topics, including but not limited to: Purely Applied Mathematics, Purified Applied Mathematics, Pure Applications in Mathematics, and Pure Applicability of Mathematics. Some highlights are the Hairy Ball Theorem, the Cox-Zucker Machine and the Ham Sandwich Theorem.
